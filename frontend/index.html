About The System

Welcome to the Hotel Procurement System – a comprehensive digital solution designed to revolutionize how hotels manage their procurement processes. This system bridges the gap between hotel administrators and vendors, creating a seamless, transparent, and efficient supply chain management platform.

Our Mission

To digitize and streamline the hotel procurement process, reducing paperwork, minimizing errors, and ensuring timely delivery of quality supplies while maintaining transparent financial records and fostering strong vendor relationships.

Features

Vendor Registration

Vendors can register online with detailed item lists, pricing, and delivery methods. All registrations are pending admin approval for quality control.

🔄 How It Works

1

Vendor Registration

Vendors submit their details, items, prices, and delivery methods for admin review.

2

Admin Approval

Admins review applications and approve qualified vendors to join the system.

3

Order Creation

Admins create purchase orders based on hotel needs and vendor offerings.

4

Delivery & Payment

Vendors fulfill orders, confirm delivery, and receive payments upon completion.
